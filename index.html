Food & Beverage

The resort has a coffee shop called the “Agotata” which is open from 6:00am to 10:00pm. Local favorites and international fare are made available throughout the day as breakfast, lunch and dinner is served here. From Filipino favorites such as adobo, crispy pata and assorted local barbecues to international choices including a variety of pastas, steaks, sea food and poultry dishes are available to ensure that guests’ gastronomic delights are satisfied.

Don’t forget to try the BBC burger and leave room for the BBC Ice Cream; definite must tries. To satisfy your thirst for a cold beverage on a hot summer day or a cup of hot coffee or tea to warm your senses, a bar is available in the restaurant. From cocktails to fresh fruit shakes, a variety of wines as well as liquor choices, our bar will make sure that your thirst will be quenched and refreshed. For guests who would rather stay in their rooms and choose to laze and relax, room service is also available from 6:00am to 10:00pm.

Club Aquasports (CASI) is a dive shop owned and operated by Bohol Beach Club. The facility is located inside the resort with a stunning beachfront which serves as take-off point to most of our offered dive attractions and destinations. Providing quality service, reliable and well maintained equipment as well as service from licensed and well trained personnel has given CASI a very good reputation with a zero record for accidents since the beginning of its operation way back in the late 1980’s. Aside from dive equipment, the shop operates its own fleet of boats made up of four (4) motorized bankas, three (3) speed boats, a glass bottom boat for leisure trips and a skiff boat for transporting personnel and equipment. Motorized and non-motorized facilities such as hobbie cats, kayaks, pedal boats and towable rides are also available for guests who are into more leisurely activities.

Special dive trips can be properly arranged in famous dive sights of Bohol such as Balicasag Island, Pamilacan Island, Cabilao Island and even Oslob, Cebu. Not to be missed are the local dives sights located within fifteen (15) minutes away from the resort namely Arco Point, Kalipayan, Habagat Wreck, Puntod and Momo. Also, right infront of the resort is a protected Marine Sanctuary which is where our famous BBC House Reef is located. PADI Certification Courses for open water, advanced open water, rescue and master scuba diver are also available, headed by two resident dive instructors.

From diving, island hopping and aquasports activities, CASI assures its clients that the service that will be offered and facilities provided shall always be of high quality with guest safety and satisfaction in mind.
